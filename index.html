The Construction Assistant Android (CAA) - Kawasaki

The CAA, Kawasaki, is a a cutting edge robotic solution to revolutionize material handling in the construction industry. This sophisticated robot features a strong base with flexible-directional foot and a dual-arm system. Engineered for handling wide range of task and precision, the dual-arm design enables the robot to highly skilled for lift and transport a heavy construction material such as metal poles. Kawasaki can be operated just by a simple controller that picks up a signal at a distance up to 30 meters. With a full charge, the CAA works for 3 days without interruption and takes aproximately 7-8 hours to charge. All of the features above allow construction companies to:

  • Increase productivity by 20% or more.
  • Reduce workplace accidents and injuries by 50% or more.
  • Improve the quality and consistency of construction outcomes.
  • Free up human workers for more complex and strategic roles.
